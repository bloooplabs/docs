## Economic Philosophy

Blooop tokenomics follows three core principles:

**1. Usage > speculation**  
Rewards come from real agent activity.

**2. Builders earn when users win**  
Skills monetize only when they create value.

**3. Infrastructure comes first**  
Treasury prioritizes security, infra, and sustainability.
